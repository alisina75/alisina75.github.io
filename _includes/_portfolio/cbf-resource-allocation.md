We develop a control-theoretic framework for solving constrained resource
allocation problems, formulated as generalized facility location with optimal
placement and assignment decisions. Leveraging Control Barrier Functions (CBFs),
Control Lyapunov Functions (CLFs), and the Maximum Entropy Principle (MEP), our
approach ensures feasibility while improving convergence and constraint handling.

This work was **submitted to CDC 2025**, with a related poster **presented at the
2025 Midwest Workshop on Control and Game Theory**.
