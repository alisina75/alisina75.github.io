Presented a poster based on our *ACC 2023 paper, "Towards Efficient Modularity in
Industrial Drying: A Combinatorial Optimization Viewpoint"*. This work addresses
the optimal sequencing and operating conditions of multiple drying technologies
with distinct mechanisms and constraints, using combinatorial scheduling to
improve energy efficiency and modular process design.
